# Product Overview

AI-powered educational platform with two core features:

1. **Exam Grading**: Upload handwritten exam papers (images/PDFs) for instant AI-powered grading with detailed feedback, transcription, and scoring
2. **Voice Tutor**: Interactive voice-based AI tutor for conversational learning and answering student questions

The application uses local AI models via Ollama (llava:13b) for privacy and offline capability, providing subject identification, language detection, answer transcription, and constructive feedback.
